# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of Uncertainty Architecture pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and productive experience for everyone. This repository is focused on **responsible AI integration, control-theory-informed patterns, and operational clarity**.
We do not tolerate harassment or exclusion.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on **ideas and artifacts**, not individuals
- Maintaining transparency and clarity in contributions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Misrepresentation of work or claims of ownership over othersâ€™ contributions
- Deliberately bypassing review processes or misusing the doctrine

---

## Responsibilities of Project Maintainers

Project maintainers (Vitalii Oborskyi, Sam) are responsible for:

- Clarifying contribution guidelines
- Reviewing contributions fairly and consistently
- Responding to code-of-conduct reports promptly
- Modeling inclusion, respect, and operational rigor

Maintainers are expected to **keep the doctrine and reference architectures clear, auditable, and safe**.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies **both within project spaces and external communication channels** (GitHub, Discord, email, or other forums related to the project).

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject contributions
that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in being
blocked from contributing.
